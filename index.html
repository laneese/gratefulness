What this is. What it isn't. How to keep it.

What is a tessera? A tessera is a single tile in a mosaic. Small, seemingly ordinary, complete in itself, and yet part of something much larger. That is what each entry here is. One small piece. The picture it's part of is yours to discover.

Your data lives here, not somewhere else. Everything you write stays on this device, in your browser's local storage. No account. No server. No company between you and your words. This journal belongs to you completely.

How to back up and restore. Because everything lives on this device, clearing your browser or switching phones means starting over unless you have a backup. In Settings, tap Export to download a single file with every entry in it. Keep that file somewhere safe. To bring your entries back, or move them to a new device, tap Import in Settings and choose that file. Export once a week and you will never lose a thing.

No subscription. Ever. This is a one-time product. There is no plan, no tier, no renewal. You paid for it once and it is yours.

How to add it to your home screen. On iPhone: tap the share button in Safari, then "Add to Home Screen." On Android: tap the three-dot menu in Chrome, then "Add to Home Screen." It will open like an app. Your data travels with your device.

One last thing. This journal is not here to make you more productive. It is not a checklist. It is not a habit tracker. It is a place to remember what is actually there, when the world is loud and you need to find the quiet again. Come back whenever you need it.
